Device configuration for Motorola Moto G57 Power
=================================================

The Motorola Moto G57 Power (codenamed **_"mumba"_**) is a mid-range smartphone from Motorola.

It was announced on **November 5, 2025** and released on **December 3, 2025**.

## Device specifications

Basic | Spec Sheet
-----:|:-------------------------
**SoC** | Qualcomm SM6435-AA Snapdragon 6s Gen 4 (4 nm)
**CPU** | Octa-core (4× Cortex-A78 @ 2.4 GHz + 4× Cortex-A55 @ 1.8 GHz)
**GPU** | Adreno
**RAM** | 8 GB / 12 GB LPDDR4X
**Storage** | 128 GB / 256 GB UFS 2.2
**Shipped Android Version** | Android 16
**Battery** | Non-removable 7000 mAh Silicon-Carbon Li-Ion
**Display** | 6.72", 120 Hz, 2400×1080 (392 ppi), IPS LCD, 1050 nits peak
**Rear Camera** | 50 MP (Wide) + 8 MP (120° Ultrawide), 1440p@30fps
**Front Camera** | 8 MP, 1440p@30fps
